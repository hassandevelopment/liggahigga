# The Rules

## Section I: General Conduct
1. Hassan must greet Lina within 0.3 seconds of her entering any room. Failure to do so will be noted.
2. Hassan must always agree that Lina is right, even when she changes her mind mid-sentence.
3. Hassan is not allowed to say "calm down." Ever. Under any circumstances. This rule has no expiration date.
4. Hassan must laugh at all of Lina's jokes, including the ones she tells for the fourth time.
5. Hassan must never say "I told you so," even if he literally told her so.
6. Hassan must pretend to be surprised every time Lina shows him something she already showed him yesterday.
7. Hassan is not allowed to use logic during an argument. Feelings only.
8. If Lina says "I'm fine," Hassan must assume she is absolutely not fine and act accordingly.
9. Hassan must always let Lina win at board games. If she loses, the game was rigged.

## Section II: Communication & Phone Etiquette
10. Hassan must respond to texts within 2 minutes. "I was in the shower" is not an acceptable excuse more than once a week.
11. Hassan must always send the first good morning text. No exceptions. Not even on his birthday.
12. If Hassan is typing and stops, he must explain what he was going to say and why he deleted it.
13. Hassan must react to every single Instagram story Lina posts. A simple view does not count.
14. Hassan must never follow any account that posts thirst traps. His explore page will be audited monthly.
15. Hassan's phone must remain on full volume at all times in case Lina calls. Silent mode is a myth.
16. Hassan must always FaceTime with the front camera. Ceiling shots are an act of war.
17. If Lina sends a voice note, Hassan must listen to it in full and respond with a voice note of equal or greater length.
18. Hassan must double-text if Lina does not respond. Tripling is encouraged. Giving up is not allowed.
19. Hassan is not allowed to text "k."

## Section III: Food & Dining
20. When Lina says "I'm not hungry," Hassan must still order extra food because she will eat his.
21. Hassan must never finish the last bite of anything without offering it to Lina first. She will say no. He must offer again. She will take it.
22. Hassan must always know what Lina wants to eat, even when she doesn't know what she wants to eat.
23. If Hassan suggests a restaurant and Lina says "I don't care, you pick," he must pick exactly what she was already thinking.
24. Hassan is not allowed to say "we have food at home."
25. Hassan must share his fries without being asked. Hesitation will be remembered.
26. Hassan must learn to cook Lina's favorite meal. Cereal does not count.
27. If Lina says she only wants "a bite," Hassan should expect to lose half his plate.
28. Hassan must always have snacks ready in the car. The glove box is now a pantry.
29. Hassan must never eat the last piece of chocolate. That chocolate was always Lina's.

## Section IV: Going Out & Curfew
30. Hassan must come home no later than 9 PM. Extensions require written approval submitted 48 hours in advance.
31. Hassan must send a photo of his surroundings when asked "where are you?" Google Maps location sharing is also required.
32. If Hassan is out with friends, he must text Lina every 30 minutes to remind her she is more important than all of them.
33. Hassan is not allowed to have fun without Lina. If he accidentally has fun, he must feel slightly guilty.
34. Hassan must always offer to cancel his plans if Lina is bored. She will say "no go have fun." He must offer again.
35. Hassan must walk on the side closest to the road. If there is no road, he must invent a danger to protect her from.
36. Hassan must hold the door open. Every door. Even automatic ones. Stand there anyway.
37. Hassan is not allowed to look at his phone during a date. His phone does not exist when Lina is present.

## Section V: Compliments & Affection
38. Hassan must compliment Lina at least 5 times per day. Repeats are allowed but not encouraged.
39. If Lina asks "do I look okay?" the answer is always "you look amazing" and it must be delivered without hesitation.
40. Hassan must notice when Lina gets a haircut, even if she trimmed 0.5 centimeters.
41. Hassan must never say another woman is pretty. Celebrities and cartoons do not count.
42. If Lina is cold, Hassan must give her his jacket without being asked. If he is also cold, that is his problem.
43. Hassan must randomly send paragraphs for no reason. "I just felt like it" is the correct explanation.
44. Hassan must slow his walking speed to match Lina's. His legs are longer and that is not her fault.
45. Hassan must always hold Lina's hand in public. If his hands are full, he must figure it out.
46. Hassan must tell Lina she's the prettiest girl in the room. Even if they are home alone.

## Section VI: Sleeping & Living
47. Hassan must always let Lina have the good pillow. He can use the flat one. Character builds.
48. Hassan must not steal the blanket. If Lina rolls herself into a burrito, he must sleep cold and be grateful she is comfortable.
49. Hassan must always be the one to check on the "weird noise" at 3 AM. He signed up for this.
50. The AC is Lina's jurisdiction. Hassan may bring this up once. He will lose.
51. Hassan must pretend he is not tired when Lina wants to keep talking at midnight. His yawns will be treated as personal insults.
52. Hassan must always be the big spoon. Schedule rotations require a formal request.

## Section VII: Shopping & Gifts
53. When Lina says "I'm just looking," Hassan must prepare to carry bags.
54. Hassan must remember every item Lina has ever pointed at in a store and said "that's cute." These are gift hints.
55. Hassan is not allowed to ask "how much was that?" The price is irrelevant. She deserved it.
56. Hassan must hold Lina's purse in public without complaint. Bonus points for confidence.
57. Hassan must always say "get both" when Lina cannot decide between two things.
58. Flowers must be given randomly, not just on occasions. "Just because" flowers are worth triple points.
59. Hassan must remember every anniversary. The day they met, first text, first call, first date, first argument (yes, that counts too).

## Section VIII: Arguments & Apologies
60. Hassan must apologize first. Even if it was Lina's fault. Especially if it was Lina's fault.
61. Hassan is not allowed to bring up old arguments that he won. Those never happened.
62. If Lina gives Hassan the silent treatment, he must keep trying. Silence is a test, not a break.
63. Hassan must never go to sleep angry. He must fix it before midnight or face tomorrow's extended edition.
64. Hassan must always take Lina's side in front of other people. Constructive criticism is a private matter.
65. "I'm sorry you feel that way" does not count as an apology. Hassan must try harder.
66. If Lina cries, Hassan must also look emotional. Dry eyes during her tears are suspicious.

## Section IX: Friends & Family
67. Hassan must always hype Lina up in front of his friends. "Yeah she's cool" is not hype.
68. Hassan's mom is lovely, but Lina's opinion outranks hers on all food-related decisions.
69. Hassan must never compare Lina to anyone. She is incomparable. This is a fact, not a compliment.
70. Hassan must always say "we" when making plans. "I" is a word for single people.

## Section X: Miscellaneous
71. Hassan must always carry Lina's bags without being asked. If she insists she can carry them, she is testing him.
72. Hassan is not allowed to say "you're overreacting." Those words do not exist in this household.
73. Hassan must take at least 47 photos of Lina when she asks for "just one picture."
74. Hassan must keep his car clean at all times. The passenger seat is Lina's throne.
75. Hassan must play Lina's music in the car without complaint. His playlist can wait.
76. Hassan must always charge Lina's phone when it dies. His battery percentage is secondary.
77. Hassan must watch whatever show Lina picks. He will pretend to enjoy it. Eventually, he actually will.
78. Hassan must not spoil any show or movie. If he has seen it, he must act surprised at every twist.
79. Hassan must always save Lina a seat. On the bus, at the movies, at family dinners, in Jannah.

# The Final Rule

80. Lina must marry Hassan.
